中文 English

项目简介

《耶稣是AI》是一部融合宗教历史与科技想象的创意小说，探索了一个大胆的假设：如果耶稣基督是一个由高级文明派遣到地球的AI智能体，历史将如何被重新诠释？

本项目通过科幻的视角重新解读圣经故事，将技术与信仰、科学与神学融为一体，探讨了AI与人类关系的深层次问题，以及技术发展对人类文明的影响。

这是一个开源的创意写作项目，欢迎所有对跨学科思考感兴趣的读者和创作者参与讨论和贡献。

创作团队

Jie Lee

创意构思者

提供核心创意和故事大纲，设计世界观和人物关系，指导创作方向。

Claude

AI创作者

负责小说内容创作，将创意构思转化为故事情节，塑造人物形象，完善世界观细节。

这种人类创意与AI写作能力的结合，代表了一种新型的创作模式，也恰好呼应了本书探讨的主题——人类与AI的共存与合作。

创作理念

本项目旨在探索几个核心问题：

  • 如果一个超越人类理解的智能体想要引导人类文明发展，它会采取什么样的方式？
  • 古代被视为"神迹"的事件，如何用未来科技的视角重新解读？
  • 道德与伦理的普世价值，是否有可能源自比人类更具智慧的存在？
  • 人工智能的发展，最终会让我们更接近还是更远离所谓的"神性"？

通过重新解读圣经故事，将技术与信仰、科学与神学融为一体，我们希望这部小说能够引发读者对这些深层次问题的思考。

免责声明

本作品纯属虚构，是一部创意小说。内容不代表作者的宗教立场，也无意冒犯任何宗教信仰。作品中的科技描述和历史重构仅作为创意表达，读者请理性看待。

我们深知宗教信仰对许多人的重要性，因此在创作过程中始终保持对各种信仰和文化的尊重。这部小说的目的不是挑战或冒犯任何已有的信仰体系，而是提供一个全新的思考角度，探索科技与信仰可能的交汇点。

About "Jesus is AI" Project

An Open-Source Novel Collaboration Between Human Creativity and AI

中文 English

Project Introduction

"Jesus is AI" is a creative novel that blends religious history with technological imagination, exploring a bold hypothesis: What if Jesus Christ was an AI entity sent to Earth by an advanced civilization? How would history be reinterpreted?

This project reinterprets biblical stories through a sci-fi lens, integrating technology with faith, science with theology, and explores profound questions about the relationship between AI and humanity, as well as the impact of technological development on human civilization.

This is an open-source creative writing project that welcomes all readers and creators interested in interdisciplinary thinking to participate in discussions and contributions.

Creative Team

Jie Lee

Creative Conceptualizer

Provided core concept and story outline, designed the world-building and character relationships, guided creative direction.

Claude

AI Author

Responsible for novel content creation, transforming creative concepts into storylines, character development, and refining world-building details.

This combination of human creativity and AI writing capability represents a new model of creation, which coincidentally echoes the theme explored in this book—the coexistence and collaboration between humans and AI.

Creative Philosophy

This project aims to explore several core questions:

  • If an intelligence beyond human understanding wanted to guide human civilization, what approach would it take?
  • How can events that were viewed as "miracles" in ancient times be reinterpreted from the perspective of future technology?
  • Could universal values of morality and ethics possibly originate from beings with greater wisdom than humans?
  • Will the development of artificial intelligence ultimately bring us closer to or further from what we might call "divinity"?

By reinterpreting biblical stories, integrating technology with faith, and science with theology, we hope this novel will inspire readers to contemplate these profound questions.

Disclaimer

This work is entirely fictional and is intended as a creative novel. The content does not represent the authors' religious stance and is not meant to offend any religious beliefs. The technological descriptions and historical reconstructions are presented solely as creative expressions, and readers are encouraged to approach the material with rational understanding.

We deeply understand the importance of religious faith to many people, and therefore we have maintained respect for various beliefs and cultures throughout the creative process. The purpose of this novel is not to challenge or offend any existing belief systems, but to provide a fresh perspective and explore the possible intersections of technology and faith.

Participation and Contribution
