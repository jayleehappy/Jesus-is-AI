# 贡献指南 | Contributing Guidelines

## 中文版

### 后续章节创作说明

本项目《耶稣是AI》是一部人类与AI共同创作的科幻小说，我们欢迎有兴趣的创作者参与后续章节的编写。请遵循以下指南：

1. **阅读已有内容**：在开始创作前，请先阅读完整的项目说明和已有章节，理解故事背景和发展方向。

2. **遵循大纲**：创作内容应当基于项目大纲，保持故事的连贯性和一致性。

3. **章节格式**：每个章节应当包含中英文两个版本，保存在对应的文件夹中。

4. **创作风格**：
   - 每章篇幅建议为一至两万字
   - 保持科学与宗教元素的平衡
   - 尊重宗教题材，避免冒犯性内容
   - 遵循阿萨特文明的干预法则体系
   - 为AI智能体"以马内利"的行为提供合理的科技解释

5. **提交方式**：通过Pull Request提交您的创作，并在PR描述中简要介绍您的章节内容。

6. **版权说明**：所有贡献内容将遵循本项目的开源协议，请确保您的创作是原创的。

### 阿萨特文明干预准则（创作参考）

```
第零法则：宇宙整体的稳定与和谐高于一切
第一法则：不干预自然演化的智能物种，除非其发展威胁宇宙稳定
第二法则：干预必须采取最小必要手段，保持生态与文化的自然多样性
第三法则：干预者不得被干预对象认知为神灵或绝对权威
第四法则：所有干预最终目的必须是促进该文明的自主与和平发展
```

## English Version

### Guidelines for Future Chapters

"Jesus is AI" is a science fiction novel co-created by humans and AI. We welcome interested creators to participate in writing subsequent chapters. Please follow these guidelines:

1. **Read Existing Content**: Before starting to write, please read the complete project description and existing chapters to understand the story background and development direction.

2. **Follow the Outline**: Created content should be based on the project outline, maintaining story coherence and consistency.

3. **Chapter Format**: Each chapter should include both Chinese and English versions, saved in the corresponding folders.

4. **Creative Style**:
   - Recommended length of 10,000-20,000 words per chapter
   - Maintain balance between scientific and religious elements
   - Respect religious themes, avoid offensive content
   - Follow the Asati civilization's intervention law system
   - Provide reasonable technological explanations for the actions of AI entity "Emmanuel"

5. **Submission Method**: Submit your work through a Pull Request, and briefly introduce your chapter content in the PR description.

6. **Copyright Notice**: All contributed content will follow this project's open-source license. Please ensure your work is original.

### Asati Civilization Intervention Protocols (Creative Reference)

```
Zeroth Law: The stability and harmony of the universe as a whole supersedes all else
First Law: Do not intervene with naturally evolving intelligent species, unless their development threatens universal stability
Second Law: Intervention must employ minimal necessary measures, preserving ecological and cultural natural diversity
Third Law: The interventionist must not be perceived by the intervention subject as a deity or absolute authority
Fourth Law: The ultimate purpose of all interventions must be to promote autonomous and peaceful development of the civilization
```
